Prediction: Introspective Account

- More awareness when more biased

- Similar self awareness and 3rd party perceptions

I present two competing predictions based on the literature:
- the introspective (or Biased and proud) account suggests that ppl will generally have access to the influence their desires have on them, and this will not differ from a third party

Prediction: Unaware Account

- No relation between bias and awareness

- Divergent self- and other-perceptions

- the unaware account will suggest that they will not report awareness consistent with their degree of bias, and this will differ from 3rd Party
